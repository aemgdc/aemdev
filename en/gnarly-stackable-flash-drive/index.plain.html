Now that’s some nice design work.

Check this out — it’s a stackable flash drive that, when you run out of capacity, you can just stack another Flash drive on top of it. You can basically have them operating in Raid 0 (well, not exactly, but similar) without having to occupy another USB port on the computer.

I thought that was beautiful.

Unfortunately, it’s just a concept at this time, but still — that’s brilliant.
