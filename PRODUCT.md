# OpsInventor Product Strategy

## Positioning

OpsInventor is positioned as an advanced technology consultancy with a strong editorial engine. The experience should signal high-value advisory capability first, while every article demonstrates depth, clarity, and strategic insight for leaders making consequential technology decisions.

**Primary register:** brand (consultancy/editorial)  
**Audience:** engineering leaders, digital transformation owners, operations executives, senior technical practitioners.

## Core Purpose

- **Demonstrate** consultancy-grade expertise through editorial depth and point of view
- **Convert** visitors from readers to advisory trust through positioning, clarity, and quality
- **Elevate** each article into a premium reading artifact, not commodity blog content
- **Preserve** editorial history and searchability (245+ articles indexed)

## Content Model

### Page Types

- **Landing (1):** Home page — introduction, article feed, navigation
- **Article (18):** Long-form editorial content with title, byline, publish date, body, tags
- **Static (1):** Statistics page — editorial material outside the main article corpus

### Navigation

- Home → Article feed / Article archive
- Articles → Tags, Search, Related articles
- Global → Header nav, Footer, Social links

## Voice

- **Tone:** Expert, strategic, precise
- **Personality:** Confident, pragmatic, deeply informed
- **IA priority:** Consultancy credibility first, article beauty and readability always

## Success Criteria

- All existing articles remain accessible and preserve editorial voice
- New design presents OpsInventor as an advanced consultancy rather than a simple blog
- Every article template reads like crafted long-form publishing with premium typography and rhythm
- Visual hierarchy supports executive scanning and deep technical reading
- Migration to AEM Edge Delivery Services preserves performance and SEO
