Style

key-points

After nearly a year using the old Regulus theme, I finally decided to take the plung and try out something new with my JetteroHeller blog. It’s odd – I’ve always been the sort of fellow who switches his Windows color scheme a million times, or is always trying out the newest GTK+ or Compiz themes on my Linux desktop. Surprising even to me that this is the first time I’ve ventured to change the look of my blog.

Well, let me know if you like it. I just got a picture of the Church of Scientology they’re making in Venezuela – a new Ideal Org for Valencia. Figured it showed what I’m trying to communicate in my blog anyhow – that people are making new things, and that this is a new world now where people can actually do something about what they want to change.
